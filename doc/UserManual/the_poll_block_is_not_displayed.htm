A user can see only those poll's questions which he/she has not answered yet.

 

That is why you can see new questions when you created them.

A browser keeps this information in its cookies. In case you cleared the cookies of your browser, you will see the questions again.

 

The same works for any user and their browsers - of course, if the cookies of their browsers are enabled.
