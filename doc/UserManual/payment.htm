Important PayPal Standard settings

1.You need to enable the option "Auto Return" in your PayPal account in order the users of your website will be returned there after making the payment using PayPal. Please refer to the p. 412 of the PayPal's "Website Payments Standard Integration Guide", May 2010.
2.If the "Currency Code" parameter is not specified in the iLister Admin Panel, the USD currency is used.
