Administrator's listings

 

Restrictions of Membership Plans and Listing Packages are not applied to listings created by administrator. Administrator can create as many listings as he/she wants with as much of pictures as desired. Administrator can apply any properties for his listings as make them Featured, Highlighted, enable YouTube video and specify rating.

 

Administrator must manually activate his listings.
