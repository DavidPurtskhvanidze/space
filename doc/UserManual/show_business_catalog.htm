show_business_catalog

 Top 

show_business_catalog - displays the Business Catalog section.

 

Parameters

•category_id - category ID of the business catalog section
•record_id - the ID of a record in the business catalog section

 

Template

business_catalog.tpl

 

Local variables

•$current_company - the array containing all information of a company
•$current_company.name - the name of a chosen company
•$current_category - a chosen category
•$current_company.full - an extended description of a company
•$GLOBALS.site_url -  the global variable containing a full URL of the site
•$records - an array with all records related to a chosen category
o$a.[field] - a temporary array containing profile fields data of a company:
o$a.name - address of the company
o$a.description - brief description of the company
o$a.email - company's contact email
o$a.url - company's site
o$a.address - company's address
o$a.phone - company's phone
o$a.fax - company's fax
o$a.full - extended description of the company
o$a.id - record ID of the company of the category
