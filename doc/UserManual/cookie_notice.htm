Description

A new EU Cookie Law came into force in the UK on 26th May 2011. The Information Commissioner’s Office (“ICO”) allowed a one year grace period prior to enforcing the law.  As of 26th May 2012, the law is to be enforced by the ICO. A fine for non-compliance determined by the ICO may reach a maximum level of £500,000.

 

Simply put, the EU Cookie Law aims to protect the privacy of internet users. It seeks to ensure that website owners who operate websites within the EU (even if the owners themselves are based outside of the EU) do the following:

1.inform users about the purpose of the cookies that their website places and stores on users’ computers; and
2.obtain users’ consent before placing and storing those cookies.

 

If you own a website in Europe – you should read the entire EU Cookie Law Guidance Notes at the UK Online Marketing Academy website.

 

 

How It Works At iLister

We developed the "Cookie Notice" module which shows the website visitors cookie notification. The module also includes the page with the explanation on what are cookies and how iLister website uses them.

 

The look of the notice can be easily changed in the design.css file at /application/modules/cookie_notice/apps/FrontEnd/templates/_files/design.css

The text can be modified in /application/modules/cookie_notice/apps/FrontEnd/templates/cookie_notice_balloon.tpl

 

You can find the module at Admin Panel -> System Configuration -> Module Manager among other modules. There you can disable the Cookie Notice module if you do not want to use it, when, for example, your website is outside of the Europe.

 

Cookie Policy In Other Languages

The module also includes the Site Page /cookie-policy/, which uses language-dependent Static Pages: CookiePolicy_en for English and CookiePolicy_ru for Russian.

 

If you plan to use other languages on your iLister-based website, please create new Static Pages for each language in Admin Panel -> Content Management -> Static Content and give them the language-corresponding names like CookiePolicy_languageID
